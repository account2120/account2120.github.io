Blood Pressure Puzzle Game

Created for Pauley Heart Center (VCU Health) by students at CodeRVA Regional Highschool

About

This educational game is designed to help students, patients, and families understand the critical aspects of blood pressure monitoring and management, delivered through an engaging, puzzle-based learning approach.

Master the four stages of blood pressure and learn effective preventative measures endorsed by cardiac professionals.

Levels in the Blood Pressure Learning Puzzle Game

  • Level I: Intro to the Heart and its functions
    This introductory module covers basic cardiac anatomy, the mechanics of blood flow, and the two key measurements that make up blood pressure: systolic and diastolic readings.
  • Level II: BP & the Dangers of Hypertension
    Explore the risks associated with high blood pressure (hypertension), including stroke, heart attack, and kidney failure. This level emphasizes why regular monitoring is vital for long-term health.
  • Level III: How to take a BP and identify High BP
    Learn the proper technique for taking blood pressure using an automated cuff. Practice reading the numbers to correctly categorize them according to current medical guidelines (Normal, Elevated, Stage 1, Stage 2).
  • Level IV: Preventative measures
    Focuses on lifestyle changes and medical interventions used to manage and prevent hypertension. Topics include diet (DASH), exercise, stress reduction, and common medication types.
  • Level V: Overview
    A comprehensive review module combining knowledge from all previous levels. This final stage tests your understanding of blood pressure monitoring and management.
